Shots Studio is a screenshots manager that helps you organize, categorize, and manage your device screenshots efficiently.

Key Features:
• Smart screenshot organization and categorization
• Advanced search and filtering capabilities
• Automatic duplicate detection and removal
• Batch operations for managing multiple screenshots
• Dark and light theme support with Material You design
• Privacy-focused with local storage
• Export and sharing options
• Background monitoring for new screenshots
• Custom collections and tagging system

Perfect for users who take many screenshots and need a better way to organize and find them. Keep your screenshot gallery clean and organized with Shots Studio's powerful management tools.

No ads, no tracking, completely free and open source.
