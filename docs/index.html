About Shots Studio

Founded in 2024, Shots Studio was born from a simple frustration: the inability to find important screenshots when you need them most. Our team of AI enthusiasts and mobile developers came together to solve this universal problem.

We believe that technology should work for you, not against you. That's why we've created an open-source solution that puts you in control of your data while leveraging the power of modern AI to make your digital life more organized.
